# Insurance-analysis-dashboard
This project presents an interactive Insurance Data Dashboard designed to analyze customer demographics, policy details, premiums, and claim performance. The dashboard transforms raw insurance data into actionable insights, helping insurance teams improve decision-making in underwriting, risk assessment, marketing, and customer management.

- Project Objectives

Analyze customer profile data such as age, gender, and policy type.

Examine premium amounts and coverage distribution across different policy categories.

Monitor claim history, claim approval patterns, and high-risk indicators.

Identify trends to support business planning and operational improvements.

Build an interactive dashboard that provides a clear view of insurance KPIs.

- Dataset Description

The dataset includes key insurance attributes such as:

Customer Information: Customer ID, gender, age

Policy Details: Policy type, start date, end date, premium amount, coverage amount

Claims Data: Claim number, claim date, claim amount, claim status

This structure allows detailed analysis of customer segments, policy performance, and claim behavior.

- Dashboard Insights

Trend of claims over time

Claim approval vs rejection analysis

Premium and coverage distribution

High-risk customer segments

Policy type performance

Relationship between age and claim amounts
